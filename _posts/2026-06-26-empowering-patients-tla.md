Modern healthcare excels at treating disease, yet patients often struggle to translate scientific evidence into practical health decisions. This article introduces the Three-Layered Approach (TLA), an evidence-based framework integrating Lifestyle Medicine, Integrative Medicine and Natural Healing to help patients make informed decisions in partnership with their healthcare professionals.  A lifestyle based approach to chronic diseases not only addresses the root cause, but also empowers patients to take charge of their journey to remission and good health. We present this with a 21-point lifestyle questionnaire in our article on Medium.
